# Product Guide: Botla-co

## Initial Concept
Botla-co is a full-stack chatbot platform designed to provide AI-powered chat capabilities for websites.

## Target Audience
The primary users of Botla-co are website owners and businesses who need an accessible way to integrate AI into their customer engagement strategy.

## Primary Goals
- **Automated Customer Support:** Provide instant, accurate responses to user inquiries using RAG-powered chatbots.
- **Lead Generation:** Capture and qualify potential customers through conversational interfaces.
- **Seamless Integration:** Offer a low-friction way to add sophisticated AI capabilities to any website.

## Core Features
- **Customizable AI Chatbots:** Leverages RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to provide context-aware responses based on specific knowledge sources.
- **Smart Actions & Handoff:** Ability to define custom tools for the bot to execute and human handoff workflows for complex queries.
- **Embeddable Chat Widgets:** A lightweight, pre-styled widget that can be easily integrated into any web page.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** A central hub for managing chatbots, monitoring performance metrics, and gaining insights from user interactions.
