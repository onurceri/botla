# Product Guidelines: Botla-co

## Messaging and Tone
- **Customizable Experience:** The platform provides users with the ability to define the tone and prose style of their chatbots via custom instructions, ensuring the AI aligns with their specific brand voice.
- **Supportive Defaults:** Default system instructions should aim for a professional and helpful tone to provide a high-quality baseline experience.

## Visual Identity
- **Dashboard:** The main dashboard application follows a clean, modern, and utility-first design philosophy, leveraging Tailwind CSS and Radix UI primitives for a professional and accessible interface.
- **Widget Customization:** The embeddable chat widget is designed to be highly customizable, allowing users to tailor its appearance to match their website's visual identity.

## User Experience Principles
- **Clarity and Ease of Use:** The dashboard should prioritize clear navigation and intuitive management of chatbots and data sources.
- **Accessibility:** Design and implementation must adhere to web accessibility standards (WCAG) where possible, ensuring a inclusive experience for all users.
